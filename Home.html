Introduction

Hello and thank you for visiting our website! We are a team of engineers at the University of Portland working for our client Tektronix!

Background

This project is solving the problem of being able to remotely access a mechanism that unites the abilities of a high-resolution camera, directional antenna, and Tektronix RSA. Their abilities will combine to allow the customer to pan 360 degrees to detect radio frequency signals and then zoom the camera in to see what is emitting those signals. The final product will include a prototype that pans the directional devices and a software package that allows its users to connect to the devices and their outputs remotely.
